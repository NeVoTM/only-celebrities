FAQ

How do I start earning on Only Celebrities?

After creating your account, upload your ID and banking details for verification. Once approved, you can publish paid content and start promoting your profile.

How do I use Only Celebrities as a creator?

The platform is packed with tools to create and share content, monetize your work, engage subscribers, and measure success — from paid messaging and livestreams to polls and analytics.

How can I make money on the platform?

Subscriptions are just the start. Earn through pay-per-view messages, posts, and streams, plus tips from subscribers — and you can even fundraise for a cause directly from your profile.

Who can join Only Celebrities?

Creators and fans must be 18+. Creators go through identity verification so the community stays authentic, safe, and trusted.
